NEI (2021 - present)


NEI is an educational company that provides professionals with online resources, continuing education, and inperson/online conferences.
I was hired to redo and maintain their website, which is very important to their users.
The main concern was that the website was largely inaccessible on small screens.

I created mock ups for everything in Adobe Xd and created all illustrations in Adobe Illustrator.
In order to create a website compatible with NEI's custom built backend system, I made everything with HTML, CSS and vanilla Javascript.
